    How to Submit

    1. 01Enter a project name and your frame range (e.g. 1-250 or a single frame like 42).
    2. 02Upload your .blend file. Max size is 512 MB. Compressed files (zstd/gzip) are supported.
    3. 03Select a priority and click Launch Render Session. The form resets and your job appears in Live Sessions.
    4. 04When rendering completes you'll get a browser notification and a download button appears on the job card.
